Types of Harassment
 

Harassment from name calling to more threatening behavior is a common part of online life that colours the experiences of many web users.

Young adults (18-29) are the most likely to experience online harassment, 70% of them 18-24. Of these individuals Young women experience severe harassment at disproportionately high levels: 26% of these young women have been stalked online, 25% were the target of online sexual harassment. This also includes physical threats and sustained harassment common to other young adults.
