Technology for Cyberbullying Detection

With the emergence of these Social Networking Sites, there has been an increase in cyberbullying circumstances, particularly among young adults. Two individuals from Malaysia, Lieu Chong Hon and Kasturi Dewi Marathon, understand why it is important to know why and how people use these tools, as they can often be used for unethical reasons by irresponsible users to cyberbully and post something in which it personally harms an individual. They have written a proposal, “Cyberbullying Detection System on Twitter,” which is a web application with the key function to effectively discover the cyberbullying related tweet from Twitter and providing reasonable solution thereafter.

With this system them users can identify the cyberbullying related tweets based on keywords and popular it in a news feed form. By doing this, it allows users to determine the identities of cyberbullies and the victims from cyberbullying tweets and will be able to help relay information to authorities based on the case’s severity.

While technology continues to grow in ways that support worldwide communication, it is important to note the negative effects it can have. It is obvious that cyberbullying will increase as the number of users and the accessibility to the Internet rises, but with the rapid advancements in technology today we can help prevent some of the severe outcomes of cyberbullying and maintain these diverse communities a positive and safe place for self-expression.
